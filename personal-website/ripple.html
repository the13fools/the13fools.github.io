Woven Ripples

Here we have a ripple, rendered in houdini. We export the mesh, and inverse weave it. These are the models we plan to fabricate.

In this installation we weave three structures. Their forms generated by a simulation of the process of a drop of water hitting a still pond. These geometries then transformed by our novel software package into fabrication instructions. These are then printed and attached to light emitting ribbons, and provide direction to weave them into their specified forms. We then control these EL ribbons with ‘Whoa Board’s, hardware that we designed and manufactured ourselves (http://whoaboard.com/store/about/).

The illumination of these ribbons (and corresponding audio) of this immersive installation responds to participant action and aims to provide a meditative space to retreat to the fundamental.

-----

Our culture is a reflection of our values. Our values direct our attention. Our attention dictates our actions.

Grown out of a deep academic exploration of basket weaving, the oft chided signifier of a meaningless pursuit - this project exists in pursuit of the futile. The ignored. The undervalued. The forgotten.

May these ripples encourage slowness, interaction, and a consideration of our planets precious water.
